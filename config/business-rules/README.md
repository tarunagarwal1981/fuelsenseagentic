# Business Rules

This directory contains business logic rules and constraints for the FuelSense 360 platform.

## Purpose

Business rules define:
- Validation constraints
- Business logic constraints
- Compliance requirements
- Optimization criteria
- Decision thresholds

## Structure

Rules should be organized by domain:
- `routing/` - Route calculation rules
- `pricing/` - Pricing and cost rules
- `compliance/` - Regulatory compliance rules
- `optimization/` - Optimization criteria
